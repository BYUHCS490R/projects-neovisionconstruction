Title and Topic

The title of my website will be Beginner Photography Guide. The topic of the site is to help beginners learn the basics of photography, including different types of cameras, fundamental rules, example shots, common mistakes to avoid, and tips for improving their skills. The site will be educational, easy to follow, and visually appealing.

Why I Chose This Topic

I chose this topic because photography is a creative skill that many people want to learn but often feel intimidated by. I enjoy taking photos myself and want to create a resource that can help beginners understand photography concepts in a simple and practical way. This topic is fun, engaging, and provides plenty of visual content to make the website interactive and interesting.

Content on Each Page

The homepage will introduce photography as a hobby and explain the goals of the website. Page 1 will cover types of cameras, including DSLR, mirrorless, point-and-shoot, and smartphone cameras, with short descriptions of each. Page 2 will cover basic photography rules, such as the rule of thirds, lighting, focus, and framing techniques. Page 3 will include example shots in a table format, showing different photography settings (aperture, shutter speed, ISO) and the resulting images. Page 4 will describe common mistakes beginners make, such as blurry shots, poor composition, and improper lighting, along with tips to avoid them. Page 5 will include a contact form where visitors can ask questions, submit their favorite photography tips, or request feedback on their own photos.
