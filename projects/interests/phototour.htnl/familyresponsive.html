Characters of the Family

The seventh Hokage

Lord Seventh is the common title for Naruto Uzumaki, who became the Seventh Hokage of the Hidden Leaf Village after the Fourth Shinobi World War. As Hokage, he is the village's leader and a powerful shinobi, known for his incredible power, perseverance, and his dream of being acknowledged by his village, which he achieved through his heroics

Kawaki Uzumaki

Kawaki is not their biological child, but Naruto and Hinata take him in like family. He was originally a test subject for the villain organization Kara. Naruto treats Kawaki with love and care, helping him learn what family means, and Kawaki forms a brotherly bond (and rivalry) with Boruto.

Henata Huga

Hinata Hyuga is Naruto Uzumaki’s wife. She comes from the noble Hyuga Clan, known for their Byakugan (a special eye technique that gives 360° vision and chakra sight). Hinata was shy, gentle, and kind-hearted since childhood, but she admired Naruto’s determination and bravery, which inspired her to become stronger.

Boruto Uzumaki

Their eldest son. He’s the main character of the sequel series Boruto: Naruto Next Generations. Boruto is talented, confident, and sometimes rebellious. He loves his father but often feels overshadowed by Naruto’s duties as Hokage.

Himawari Uzumaki

Their younger daughter. She’s sweet, caring, and very close to her family. Even though she’s young, she inherits the Byakugan and shows great potential.
