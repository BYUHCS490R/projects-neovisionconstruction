Anime

Anime is one of my all-time favorites to watch. I enjoy how it has good stories, good characters, and good artwork. A few of my all-time favorite anime include One Piece, Demon Slayer, Attack on Titan, Dragon Ball, Berserk, and Jujutsu Kaisen. They all have something unique about them such as adventure, friendship, or epic fights. Anime makes me happy and relaxed after a long day.

Anime also reminds me of numerous awesome lessons regarding being brave, working together, and never giving up. I like the fact that the characters always do their best, even when things get overwhelmingly difficult. It also teaches a lot about Japanese culture, which I find very fascinating to learn about. Anime is not a cartoon to me it's a way to envision new worlds and become inspired.
